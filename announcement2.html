Subject: FSA and HSA Legislation Change

Recently passed legislation, in response to COVID-19, has included over-the-counter (OTC) medicine and medical supplies, including menstrual care products as
qualified medical expenses for FSA and HSA accounts. If you currently have a FSA or HSA account, you may now claim OTC medicine and medical supplies
beginning retroactively to January 1, 2020 and for the indefinite future (no end date was provided). IRS has not yet published a list of OTC medicines and supplies,
but that should be out soon.
