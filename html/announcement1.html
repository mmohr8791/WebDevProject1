Subject: Washable and Preventive Masks

In the likelihood that our various States and/or Counties may require the use of personal preventive masks while out in public, RTP Company sought out a local manufacturer to make
personal, washable, preventive masks as a benefit for each of our employees.

These masks are non-medical grade and are not personal protective equipment (PPE) and cannot replace the required N95 masks or respirators when working in areas that require such.
The masks are reusable and launderable with general laundry detergent (no bleach). The material is tightly woven with a high-end anti-microbial material and an elastic band to firmly
fit the mask around oneâ€™s face and nose.

These masks should arrive by Monday, April 27, 2020 and we will begin distribution to the various US facilities and departments upon arrival. Employees are welcome to pick up a
courtesy mask from their plant manager or department manager. Employees may also choose to make their own masks. Please see attached flyer from CDC on how to make your own
masks with t-shirt or bandana material.

Thank you and please continue to adhere to social distancing efforts as we fight this pandemic.
